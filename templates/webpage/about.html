Motivation

Efficient tender management is crucial for organizations to streamline procurement processes, reduce errors, and enhance collaboration among stakeholders.

Proposed Solution

The proposed tender management system offers a centralized platform for managing tender processes from issuance to contract awarding. Key features include:

  • Centralized information hub for tender details
  • Online tender requests and submissions
  • Alerts and notifications
  • Comprehensive reporting

Objectives

The objectives of the tender management system are:

  • Improving tender purchasing and procurement processes
  • Ensuring efficient process for both buyers and sellers
  • Facilitating easy document management
  • Implementing systematic bid assessment
  • Reducing likelihood of poor submissions

For Buyers (Companies)

Buyers can:

  • Register on the platform
  • Create profiles with organization and procurement details
  • Create and configure tender opportunities
  • Define tender details and evaluation criteria

For Bidders (Companies Offering Tendering Services)

Bidders can:

  • Register on the platform
  • Create profiles with company details and certifications
  • Receive notifications about tender updates
  • Access feedback and evaluation results
  • Upload tender documents
